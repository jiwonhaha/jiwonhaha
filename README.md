## 🚀 About Me
I'm a Engineer and developer focused on **3D computer vision and graphics** — Gaussian Splatting, neural rendering, and pose estimation. I enjoy turning cutting-edge research into working systems and writing about the papers that shape the field.

- 🎓 **MSc Machine Learning**, University College London (UCL) — *Distinction*
- 🎓 **BSc Computer Science**, University of Birmingham — *First-Class Honours*
- ✍️ I write **paper reviews on 3D vision & rendering** at [jiwonhaha.github.io](https://jiwonhaha.github.io)
- 🔬 Interests: 3D Gaussian Splatting, Novel View Synthesis, DUSt3R-style reconstruction, Diffusion Models

---

## 🔭 Featured Projects
- **[GaussCraft](https://jiwonhaha.github.io/gausscraft/)** — 2D Gaussian Splatting editing via mesh deformation. *(Inspired by 2DGS and Gaussian Avatar)*
- **[METR](https://github.com/jiwonhaha/METR)** — Non-Human Primate pose estimation with a Transformer-based model, supporting animal behavior analysis.
- **[Passport Control Simulator](https://github.com/jiwonhaha/Passport-control-simulator)** — VR passport-control training system for Oculus Quest 2, built in Unity & C#.
